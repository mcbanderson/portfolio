About Me

My name is Max Anderson, and I am a professional Software Engineer, DevOps Engineer, and Web Developer working in the Sacramento, CA area.

I got started in the industry the same way many people do, by creating a new WordPress website for the company I was working for. Though you can hardly call what I was doing then engineering, I quickly bacame hooked and have never looked back. All these years later I've built everything from simple static websites to custom, fully automated Cloud Security systems for Fortune 500 clients. I have developed games, mobile apps, REST APIs, and database driven applications. And I look forward to adding to that list in the coming years.

In addition to my day job, I am currently pursuing a MS in Computer Science from Georgia Tech through their OMSCS program. Before attending Georgia Tech I received a BA in Economics from UC Davis, and an AS in Computer Science from Sacramento City College.
