About Me

My name is Max Anderson, and I am a professional Software Engineer, Website Designer, and Website Developer working in the Sacramento, CA area.

I am currently pursuing a MS in Computer Science from Georgia Tech through their OMSCS program. Before attending Georgia Tech I received a BA in Economics from UC Davis, and an AS in Computer Science from Sacramento City College.

I have years of experience creating beautiful, responsive websites. My experience includes everything from simple informational websites built using WordPress, to completely custom, database driven web and mobile applications. I also has experience building accessible websites, which ensures that website content can be accessed by everyone, including people with various dissabilities.

InnovaNutra

Website Design and Construction

I designed and built the website for InnovaNutra, a bio-tech startup located in Davis, CA. InnovaNutra is developing natural ingredients which can be used in food, beverage, and skincare products. To reflect the natural aspect of their products, the design includes bright, colorful food ingredients to represent InnovaNutra's offerings.

Association of Women's Business Centers
